Free Online Teleprompter for iPad and Tablet

WebPrompter is a simple, free teleprompter app that runs directly in your browser. It turns your browser into a professional autocue studio without requiring any software downloads.

  • Remote Control: Use your iPhone or Android phone to control speed and scroll wirelessly.
  • Mirror Mode: Flips text for use with professional teleprompter glass / beamsplitters.
  • Offline Capable: Works on iPad, tablet, laptop, and desktop (Windows/Mac).
